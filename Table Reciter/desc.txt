This skill will recite the multiplication table of any number you say for you and will help you learn it.
Open this skill by saying "Alexa open table reciter" and then to recite a table of any number like four say utterances like 
"say table of four", "times table of four" or "table of four".
You can also repeat the table by just saying "repeat" and close the skill by saying "close" or "stop".
